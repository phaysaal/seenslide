## Design Principles

1. **Simplicity:** Eye should be recognizable even at small sizes
2. **Scalability:** Works from 16x16 favicon to billboard
3. **Memorable:** Distinctive shape, easy to recall
4. **Professional:** Suitable for academic/corporate contexts
5. **Meaningful:** Directly relates to "seeing" slides

---

## File Formats Needed

- **Vector:** SVG (scalable for all sizes)
- **Raster:** PNG (transparent background)
  - 16x16, 32x32, 64x64, 128x128, 256x256, 512x512
- **Favicon:** ICO (for browser tabs)
- **Social:** 1200x630 for Open Graph
